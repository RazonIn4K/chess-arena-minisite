Safety & Community

Fair play, fast games, zero gatekeeping. We're here to make chess + gaming more fun.

Our Community Principles

Competitive but Inclusive

Celebrate skill and improvement, not elitism. We say "Get better" not "git gud." Acknowledge frustration without being toxic.

Nerdy but Accessible

Use gaming/chess terms, but explain them. Assume smart audience, not expert audience.

Playful but Respectful

Memes and humor are welcome. No punching down—no skill-shaming, no gatekeeping. Self-aware about the absurdity of "chess MOBA."

Zero Tolerance

Hate, harassment, and cheating have no place in our community. We will act swiftly on reports.

  • • Hate speech — Instant ban, no warnings
  • • Harassment — Including targeted abuse, doxxing, brigading
  • • Cheating — Using exploits, bots, or unauthorized tools
  • • Gatekeeping — "Only [X rating]+ will understand" rhetoric

Community Guidelines

✅ Do This

  • • Use gender-inclusive language ("players," "folks")
  • • Welcome newcomers and explain jargon
  • • Share tactical tips and celebrate improvement
  • • Report bugs privately to playtest team
  • • Disclose sponsored/paid content (FTC compliance)

❌ Don't Do This

  • • Use toxic slang ("hardstuck," "skill issue" unironically)
  • • Shame players for rating or experience
  • • Share unreleased builds or invite codes
  • • Stream snipe or intentionally disrupt matches
  • • Use gendered or exclusive language ("chess bros")

In-Game Conduct

  • Fair Play: No exploits, bots, or unauthorized tools. We log all matches for review.
  • Matchmaking: Respect queues—no stream sniping or targeted harassment.
  • Bug Reports: Found an issue? Report privately via playtest@chessarena.gg
  • Content Creation: Streams and videos are welcome! Tag @chessarenagame for featuring.

Legal & Compliance

FTC Disclosure: Content creators must disclose sponsored or paid placements. Use #ad or "Sponsored by Chess Arena" in posts.

COPPA Compliance: Chess Arena is intended for ages 13+. Players under 13 require parental consent.

Privacy: We collect minimal data for matchmaking and anti-cheat. See our Privacy Policy.

DMCA: Respect copyright. Don't use unlicensed music or art in streams/videos.
